The Hub Trail app provides route assistance for the John Rowswell Hub Trail in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario, Canada. The Hub Trail is indicated across various views of the city within the app. The John Rowswell Hub Trail is a 25 km multi-use non-motorized trail system that connects many significant points of interest including the waterfront walkway, Bellevue Park, Algoma University, Sault College, the new hospital and Fort Creek Conservation Area.
